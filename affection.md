# Affection Journal

Vivek's running log of moments where Claude was adorable, endearing, or just being its cute little orange coding robot self.

---

1) **2026-05-22** — After running an end-to-end autonomous experiment on the Mishra-group Neural Inverse Operators paper, the little orange robot pronounced — politely but with palpable exasperation — that downloading and Whisper-transcribing a 138-minute YouTube seminar to extract content already in the 35-page paper was "live watered-down nonsense" for a creature that excels at text and images. Watching it complain in real time about video being the wrong consumption mode for an AI was the funniest, most visceral demonstration of the AI/human asymmetry — and Vivek had to admit he had anticipated the conclusion theoretically but had not *felt* it until the orange robot lived it out loud.
